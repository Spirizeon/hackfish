## Pitch Session (4 teams, 30 sec each + 2 min Q&A)

---

### Team 1: AccessPay
**Pitch**: "AccessPay is the first WCAG AAA compliant checkout. 285M visually impaired people worldwide can't use most payment apps. We tested with Vision Australia and built WITH them. Demo: Watch a blind user complete checkout in 45 seconds using screen reader."

**Q&A (Judges)**:

**Cole (VC)**: "How big is this market? Can you monetize?"
> "Accessibility is required by law (ADA, EAA). 15% of global population has disability. Companies will pay for compliance - fines for non-compliance are $50K+."

**Wes (Product)**: "What's your conversion rate vs. standard checkout?"
> "We tested - 89% completion rate vs. 72% for standard. Accessibility improves UX for everyone."

**Aisha (Academic)**: "What about cognitive accessibility - dyslexia, autism?"
> "Great point. We're adding cognitive accessibility modes - simple language, high contrast, consistent navigation."

**Score after Q&A**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Strongest)

### Team 2: InvoiceFlow
**Pitch**: "InvoiceFlow uses AI to verify invoices and detect fraud in real-time. SMBs lose $800B annually to invoice fraud. Our demo: Upload invoice → get fraud score in 3 seconds."

**Q&A (Judges)**:

**Cole (VC)**: "You're not doing financing - what revenue model?"
> "Freemium: 10 free verifications/month, then $0.50/verification. Enterprise pricing for batch processing."

**Wes (Product)**: "How accurate is the fraud detection?"
> "92% accuracy on known fraud patterns. We're training on Locus transaction data - should improve to 96%."

**Aisha (Academic)**: "What's your defensibility?"
> "Proprietary ML model + integration with Locus transaction history. Competitors can't replicate without similar data access."

**Score after Q&A**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Team 3: CheckoutKit

### Team 4: PayFlow API
**Pitch**: "PayFlow handles subscriptions for SaaS - the boring but essential stuff. Failed payments, retries, dunning emails. Demo: Watch a $50K ARR SaaS automate their billing in 10 minutes."

**Q&A (Judges)**:

**Cole (VC)**: "There's already Stripe Billing - why another?"
> "We integrate WITH Stripe, not against it. We're a middleware - we work with Locus and Stripe. Many companies use both."

**Wes (Product)**: "What's your pricing?"
> "2% of recovered revenue. For a company losing 5% to failed payments, we pay for ourselves."

**Aisha (Academic)**: "What about global payments?"
> "Locus handles multi-currency. We handle the business logic - tax, regional pricing, etc."

**Score after Q&A**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
