### AccessPay (Team 1)
**5-min Pitch**

*"Every year, 285 million visually impaired people are excluded from the digital economy. Not because they can't use technology - but because we didn't design for them.*

*AccessPay is the first WCAG AAA compliant checkout. We built WITH Vision Australia - not for them.*

**The Problem**: 
- 85% of payment apps fail basic accessibility tests
- ADA non-compliance lawsuits up 300%
- SMBs don't know how to make their checkouts accessible

**Our Solution**:
- Drop-in accessibility wrapper for Locus Checkout
- Real-time screen reader optimization
- Voice command checkout option

**The Demo**:
*Shows 45-second checkout using VoiceOver on iOS*

**Market**:
- $27B accessibility software market
- 15% of world population has disability
- Compliance is legally required

**Business Model**:
- $99/month for SMB tools
- 0.5% transaction fee
- Enterprise pricing

**Why Now**:
- EAA (European Accessibility Act) mandates accessibility by 2025
- ADA enforcement increasing
- Locus has accessibility APIs we can build on

**We're not just building for disabled users - we're building better checkout for everyone."*

**Q&A**:

> "Next: cognitive accessibility (dyslexia, autism), motor disabilities. The framework scales."

### InvoiceFlow (Team 4)
**5-min Pitch**

*"SMBs lose $800 billion annually to invoice fraud. That's not just numbers - it's businesses going bankrupt.*

*InvoiceFlow uses AI to verify invoices and detect fraud in real-time.*

**The Problem**:
- Invoice fraud is the #1 crime against SMBs
- Traditional fraud detection costs $50K+ / year
- SMBs can't afford enterprise solutions

**Our Solution**:
- Upload invoice → get fraud score in 3 seconds
- Three-signal detection: metadata, cash flow (via Locus), ML
- 92% accuracy, 4% false positives

**The Demo**:
*Shows live invoice upload → fraud score of 0.87 → highlights red flags*

**Market**:
- $12B fraud detection market
- 30M US SMBs issue invoices
- Regulatory tailwinds: stronger verification requirements

**Business Model**:
- Freemium: 10 verifications/month free
- $0.50/verification after
- Enterprise: $999/month unlimited

**Why Now**:
- Locus transaction data enables unprecedented accuracy
- AI models are finally good enough
- Regulatory pressure increasing

**We're not just fraud detection - we're building trust in B2B transactions."*

---

**Q&A**:

**Cole**: "How do you get data to train your model?"
> "We have partnerships with 3 invoice factoring companies - they provided 10M labeled invoices. Plus synthetic data generation."

**Wes**: "What happens when fraudsters adapt?"
> "Continuous learning - we retrain weekly. Locus transaction data gives us real-time signals they can't fake."

**Aisha**: "International expansion plan?"
> "Model is language-agnostic - we analyze structure, not text content. Currency handled by Locus."
