### AccessPay (Team 1)
**5-min Deep Dive**

**Cole (VC)**: "Walk me through your business model. How do you price?"
> "B2B: $99/month for accessibility compliance tools. Enterprise: custom. Plus we take 0.5% transaction fee. For a company doing $1M/month, that's $6K/year. Still 10x cheaper than non-compliance lawsuits ($50K+)."

**Cole**: "What's your customer acquisition strategy?"
> "Partner with accessibility compliance consultants. They already sell to companies needing ADA compliance. Referral model - 30% commission."

**Wes (Product)**: "What's your technical architecture?"
> "React frontend + Node.js backend. We built a screen-reader-optimized wrapper around Locus Checkout SDK. We had to extend their accessibility APIs - they were supportive."

**Wes**: "Can you show me the demo again? Specifically the error handling?"
> *Shows live demo with invalid card input - screen reader announces error clearly, suggests correction*

**Aisha (Academic)**: "Your sample size was 20 users. How do you validate at scale?"
> "We're partnering with Vision Australia for a 200-user study. We'll publish the results as a white paper - good for credibility."

**Aisha**: "What's your differentiator vs. existing accessibility tools?"
> "Those tools audit AFTER you build. We integrate accessibility INTO the checkout flow. You can't NOT be accessible with us."

**Judge Assessment**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### InvoiceFlow (Team 4)
**5-min Deep Dive**

**Cole (VC)**: "You're pivoting from financing to verification. What's the TAM?"
> "Invoice verification: $12B market (fraud detection software). We're targeting the 30M SMBs in the US who issue invoices."

**Cole**: "Why not go back to financing? That's bigger."
> "Compliance overhead is massive. You'd need banking licenses in 50 states. Verification is software - we can scale without regulatory burden."

**Wes (Product)**: "How do you detect fraud? What signals?"
> "Three signals: (1) Invoice metadata anomalies - amounts, dates, vendor history; (2) Locus transaction data - cash flow patterns; (3) ML model trained on 10M labeled invoices."

**Wes**: "What's your accuracy vs. false positive rate?"
> "92% accuracy, 4% false positive. We show the confidence score so users can override."

**Aisha (Academic)**: "What about international invoices?"
> "Locus handles multi-currency. Our fraud model works globally - we're training on international invoice datasets."

**Judge Assessment**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
